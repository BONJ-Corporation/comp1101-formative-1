Wearable Technology in Healthcare

Wearable tech for the visually impaired

Researchers have created a wearable computer to aid the blind, and help reduce collision risk.
The computer consists of a small mainboard and several cameras which allow it to detect objects around the wearer, when used in conjunction with a guide dog, it is reportedly 40% effective at preventing collisions
